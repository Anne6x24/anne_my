GAME EXPLAINATION

giddy up is about a cowboy hos lost all of his ten sheriff stars, therefore the player needs to help him getting them back without losing three/three lives. to win the player needs to collect all ten. the stars are moving by on a conveyor belt alongside some other animations. if the player clicks on other animations than the stars the player loses a life
