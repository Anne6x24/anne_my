"DISCLMR is a copenhagen based design, fashion and view-pointing brand founded in early 2021, directed by multimediadesigner anne my munksgaard. CLMR is a word-forming element meaning “to claim”. in other words can CLMR be described as the process of claiming message, a worldview, ideology or a stand. “claim your view of point without disclaiming it”, is our mantra and the foundation of our brand as well as our focus when dropping different topics along side each collection." click the link below to watch the final work

SKETCH

"the overall CLMR of our brand is our aim to reinvent the traditional industry towards more environmental ways as well as muting waste-culture.

we wish to create from what already is, and thereby avoid adding new fabrics in to the world. by contributing to the idea of a greener world, we also claim the rights for environmental activism.

all products are handpicked from stockits around the world and upcycled before sale by our amazing team. each drop is limited pieces, and a new drop will first show when most is sold.

this brand is our activism against waste-culture"
